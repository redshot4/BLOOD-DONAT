Donor Health Information

Your safety is our priority. Please review these essential guidelines.

Basic Requirements

⚖️

Age & Weight

  • At least 18 years old
  • Min weight: 110 lbs (50 kg)
  • No upper age limit if healthy
❤️

Vitals

  • Systolic BP: ≤ 180 mmHg
  • Diastolic BP: ≤ 100 mmHg
  • Pulse: 50-100 bpm
🩸

Hemoglobin

  • Men: ≥ 13.5 g/dL
  • Women: ≥ 12.5 g/dL
  • Tested on-site before donation
⚠️

Disqualifiers

  • Active cold, flu, or fever
  • Recent surgery (last 6 mo)
  • Pregnancy or recent birth
  • Low iron levels

Preparing for Your Donation

✓ Do

  • Get 7-9 hours of sleep the night before
  • Eat a healthy, iron-rich meal
  • Drink an extra 16oz of water
  • Bring a valid government photo ID
  • Wear a shirt with sleeves that can be rolled up

✗ Don't

  • Don't drink alcohol 24hrs before
  • Don't smoke for 1 hour before
  • Don't eat fatty foods immediately before
  • Don't donate if you feel unwell
  • Don't take aspirin 48hrs before (for platelets)

After Donation Care

Immediately

  • Rest in the canteen for 15 mins
  • Drink juice and eat a snack
  • Keep bandage on for 4 hours

Next 24 Hours

  • Drink 4 extra glasses of water
  • Avoid heavy lifting or exercise
  • Eat iron-rich meals

Monitoring

  • If you feel dizzy, lie down
  • If bruising occurs, apply ice
  • Report any post-donation illness

Boost Your Iron

Eat these foods regularly to maintain healthy iron levels
